For years, we've been prisoners of the infinite scroll. Our attention, a commodity to be harvested, was fed into the insatiable maw of endless content. But this model, born of _The Old Web_, is fundamentally flawed. It treats users as passive consumers, their agency limited to the binary choice of scrolling up or down.

Instead of a linear feed, we have a dynamic, interactive environment where content has position, velocity, and relationships to other elements.
